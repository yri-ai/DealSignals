**Member churn rate** is the percentage of members who **leave/cancel during a given period**.

A common way to calculate it is:

\[
\text{Member Churn Rate}=\frac{\text{Members lost during the period}}{\text{Members at the beginning of the period}}
\]

(often expressed as a %).
