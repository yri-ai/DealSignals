## SoftBank's Role and Ongoing Obligations

### 1. **Major Stockholder Status**
SoftBank entities (including SBG, SBWW, and SVFE) are major stockholders of WeWork and will continue to be substantial stockholders of New WeWork following the Business Combination. SBWW is described as "WeWork's largest stockholder" (page v).

**Confidence: HIGH**

### 2. **Debt Financing Arrangements**
As of June 30, 2021, SoftBank's debt-related commitments to WeWork include:
- **$1.7 billion** in outstanding letters of credit under the 2020 LC Facility
- **$2.2 billion** in outstanding indebtedness under the SoftBank Senior Unsecured Debt
- Ability for WeWork to borrow up to **$1.1 billion** under the SoftBank Senior Secured Debt (subject to restrictive covenants)

(Page 40, Risk Factors section)

### 3. **Credit Support Letter Agreement**
SoftBank Obligor has committed to consent to an extension of the termination date of the 2020 LC Facility from February 10, 2023 to no later than February 10, 2024 (the "LC Facility Termination Extension"). This extension requires requisite consent of lenders. (Page 7)

**Confidence: HIGH**

### 4. **Commitment Letter for Secured Notes**
StarBright WW LP (an affiliate of SBG) agreed to purchase up to **$550 million** of 7.50% senior secured notes from WeWork Companies LLC. These notes will mature on the earlier of: (a) 18 months from Closing, or (b) February 12, 2023. (Page 7)

**Confidence: HIGH**

**Confidence: HIGH**
